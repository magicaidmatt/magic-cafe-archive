I don't usually make any New Year's Resolutions, but this year I have resolved to undertake my most ambitious project ever... I am publishing my masterworks! I plan to release three large books which will contain (just about) everything I've ever created in magic. Each book will be approximately 300 pages (maybe more) and cover 18 years of creations (54 years total! Yup, I'm old!). I'll release a book every two years. There... I've committed to it. There's no turning back.

So, over the next six years, I will be releasing three fully-illustrated, full-color books, each covering 18 years of my original creations, consisting of 300-400 pages, containing routines, scripts, stories, photographs, essays, plans, philosophy, principles, worksheets, ideas, augmented reality videos, etc. There are a number of ways to support this massive project:

Each book is priced at $100 retail, $180 for two books, or $250 for the set of three.

Instead of “pre-orders,” I am taking SUBSCRIPTIONS, which entitle you to receive incremental PDF installments as I complete them, as well as the finished book(s). If you subscribe to one, two, or all three books, you receive these installments. Extra-special perks are reserved for those who subscribe to all three, as follows:

The first 99 to subscribe to all three books will receive autographed, numbered editions (in order of subscription), and a display box for the full set.

The first 66 to subscribe to all three books will also be thanked in the credits of all three books, and receive autographed iconic artwork related to each book.

The first 33 to subscribe to all three books will have their autographed artwork framed, and receive an additional autographed Card-Toon card from the deck used to fool Penn&Teller!

Additionally, if you’d like to become a sponsor of this project, you can contact me (contact@danharlanmagic.com) about one-of-a-kind collectibles and other perks.

To subscribe to this project, please visit…
https://themystictower.com/shop/ols/prod......terworks
