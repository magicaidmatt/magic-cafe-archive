A short sad story that relates. A magician friend of mine made his living solely from his gigs. We would often share gigs if either was booked and would work together at gigs as well.

Then Covid hit. We talked about the situation and how he was going to supplement his income. He seemed fine to me and we joked about the situation. What else can you do? I found out not too long afterwards that my friend committed suicide.

I couldn't believe it. (If only) If only I would have called more or go out to lunch to talk or... Sadly these tragedies have a similar theme. I think of Daryl, happy go lucky or so it seemed and took his life. Yes like Robert mentions that it is so important to reach out and care.

This Christmas season offers a saving message to embrace and think about.
Guilt will betray you before technique betrays you!
