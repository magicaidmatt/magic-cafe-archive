If you havent written one, you don't have slightest idea how hard job it is! Its not just the writing, that is the easy part! But you have to write it multiple times, since rewriting is a huge part of it. Each rewrite makes the book easier to read and understand. Also you often use people to comment on "finished chapters" and those comments usually lead into adjustments. Proofreading is also a thing and takes time. If you don't have proofreading done well you get spammed by people who found errors in your book.

Do you use photos or pictures. Both need to be made and edited and can take much much longer than expected.

Layout for book also requires time and skill. Writing a book that is printed is not just typing a word document...

And the there is printing. First single or few copies to check everything looks good, then final printing, often abroad since it is so much cheaper but takes easily 20-45 days just to print, then ship to your country etc.

But again, people who have never done anything like this don't have an idea how much work making a book can be... And the profit for even 100$ book is actually very very very little considering that most magic books sell maybe 500-1000 copies at most and take easily hundrets of hours to write and make.

Juha-Matti
